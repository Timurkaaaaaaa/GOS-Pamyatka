Jurisdiction:

District jurisdiction marked in red belongs to LSCSD, SANG.

The city jurisdiction is marked in blue and belongs to LSPD, USSS, USMS.

The FIB, in the performance of its official duties, has the right to operate throughout the territory of the state..

The FIB has the right to issue a citation for violation of jurisdiction in the event of ignoring the department into whose zone the passage is carried out.

Employees are allowed to violate jurisdiction after 10:00 PM and before 12:00 AM.

Employees have the right not to request permission to enter another jurisdiction if a military supply or raid operation is being carried out.

Stages of the use of force:

• Presence

• Requirements, orders

• Use of physical force

• Use of special means (electric shocker, baton, etc.)

• Use of lethal weapons

Detention process:

Detention:

• Put handcuffs on the suspect

• Identify yourself

• Initial search

• Explain the reason for the detention

• Read the Miranda Rule

• Deliver to interrogation room

• Identify the detainee

• Check affiliation with government agencies

• Realization of rights. If the detainee is a civil servant, call the authorities and the prosecutor's office

• Investigative actions (interrogation, examination, etc.) if necessary

• Arrest

Arrest:

• Secondary search

• Seizure of illegal items

• Update search priority

• Explain the reason for the arrest if necessary

• Place in camera

Reason for detention:

• The person was caught in the act of committing a crime

• There are clear signs of a crime on the clothes.

• There is photo/video recording of violations

• There is a document ordering detention

• The person is wanted

Grounds for release:

• The suspicion of committing a crime was not confirmed

• There is no penalty in the form of imprisonment for the violation.

• There are no grounds for detention

• The detention lasts more than 60 minutes
